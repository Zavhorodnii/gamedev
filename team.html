RFJ Tokenomics and Use Cases

How can Jollity help game developers?

Ecosystem stakeholders will be able to vote on the following questions:

  • 1) Should a particular game receive funding from the fund for its in-game tournaments?
  • 2) How should the fund be used to reward RFJ holders and gamers in our ecosystem?
  • 3) Should the fund be invested in the development of future products proposed by the Jollity team?
