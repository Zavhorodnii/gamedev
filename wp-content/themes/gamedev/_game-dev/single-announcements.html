Connecting games

In the two weeks since the last DAO Committee report, seven points of interest were added to the Decentraland map, multiple polls were passed focusing on improving in-world user experience, and three grants were approved and funded.

Over the coming weeks, the DAO will release a strategic plan aiming to increase governance engagement and will be hosting the first town hall on Thursday November 11, 2021, led by Matimio, the recently hired DAO Facilitator. All community members are invited to participate in this general interest session, which will also be attended by at least one DAO Committee member. Details on the event will be published on the Decentraland Events page in the coming days.
