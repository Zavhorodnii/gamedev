RFJ Tokenomics and Use Cases

RFJ use cases

Play-to-earn

Complete tasks in games with your NFT items and enter tournaments on Jollity to earn RFJ tokens.

Governance

Ecosystem stakeholders will be able to vote on fund distributions.

Staking

Rewards will be affected by how often you use your game-NFT in-game, as well as how often you vote on ecosystem fund management proposals.

Ecosystem Fund

Ecosystem stakeholders will be able to vote on fund distributions.

Payment

Jollity users can use RFJ as a unit of account when buying, selling, and renting in-game NFT items, as well as in tournaments in the Light Hand event.
