First step towards the Tokenezation of the gaming industry

We are building a decentralized ecosystem for players and Non-blockchain games

For Game Developers

Tokenization of in-game items will allow game developers to open up new ways to monetize through the sales of valuable and limited-edition in-game items from their game.

Many games will be able to discover and monetize the secondary market, generating income from the resale of game items between users.

Games will be able to increase their user base.

For Gamers

If you're a gamer, all in-game items you buy or get in the game will be stored in your wallet as NFT, not in their in-game account, which can be frozen by developers or stolen by intruders.

The ability to have full control over your game assets gives you the freedom to buy, sell, rent collect, and exchange your assets.

Monetize your time in the game and enjoy.

Platform Features

Marketplace of in-game NFT items with a 4% commission on NFT sales.

Personal stores for game developers completely under their control.

Tournaments for players and Play-to-earn model to enable users to monetize their time in the game using their in-game NFT items.

Leagues to unite gamers and get more valuable prizes.

Reward For Joy

One of the goals of our project is to create a community of gamers and game development companies to enable both of them to earn with our platform's native token, simply by playing games. The RFJ token "Reward For Joy" is limited to a maximum of 320M, which will be gradually unlocked over 54 months from public sale in fall 2022 on a predetermined schedule.

Private sales
5% (16,000,000) of the total number of RFJ tokens are allocated for sale to strategic investors.
  • Investors in the private sale will purchase RFJ at a 20% discount.
  • Funds raised will go toward developing the MVP version of the site, marketing, and preparing for a public sale in Fall 2022.
  • All tokens from the private sale will be gradually unlocked and available to investors according to the following schedule:
  1. 20% during the public sale (Fall 2022).
  2. Then gradual unlocking over 24 months: 40% in 2023 (end of Q1 and Q3) and 40% in 2024 (end of Q1 and Q3).

Ecosystem Fund

+
Ecosystem Fund
The ecosystem fund is built on three principles:
  1. Encourage RFJ holders who lock in their coins and manage the fund.
  2. Enable game developers to create their tournaments with rewards in RFJ to be allocated from the ecosystem fund.
  3. Improve methods of rewarding players through play-to-earn.

The fund will be continually replenished by having 85% of the RFJ from the amount users spend at Light Hand sent to the fund, as well as from the sale of game NFT items on the marketplace for RFJ - (3.5% Jollity commission).

Initially, the fund will hold 8% of the total RFJ, which will be gradually unlocked over 34 months under the Jollity team. Every expenditure from the fund will be available for public inspection through the https://bscscan.com/ and reflected in quarterly reports on community treasury expenditures.
